How Many Porta Potties Do I Need? OSHA Requirements & Event Planning Guide

Planning Published 2026-03-07 • Updated March 2026

Porta Potty Calculator: How Many Do You Need?

Quick Answer: OSHA requires 1 toilet seat per 20 workers on construction sites. Events need 1 porta potty per 50 guests for events under 4 hours. Adjust up for alcohol, long events, or majority female attendance.

Construction Sites (OSHA Requirements)

OSHA Standard 1926.51 mandates specific toilet facilities for construction sites in Atlanta and nationwide:

For Sites with 1-20 Workers

  • 1 toilet seat required (can be single porta potty)
  • Service at least once per week
  • Must be within 5 minutes walking distance

For Sites with 20-200 Workers

  • 1 toilet seat and 1 urinal per 40 workers
  • Example: 80 workers = 2 toilet seats + 2 urinals (can use 2 standard porta potties + 1 urinal-only unit)
  • Service twice per week recommended

For Sites with 200+ Workers

  • 1 toilet seat and 1 urinal per 50 workers
  • Example: 250 workers = 5 toilet seats + 5 urinals
  • Service 3 times per week required

Events & Gatherings

Basic Formula: 1 porta potty per 50 guests for events up to 4 hours

Events by Size (4-hour duration)

  • 50 guests: 1-2 units (2 recommended for buffer)
  • 100 guests: 2-3 units
  • 200 guests: 4-5 units
  • 500 guests: 10-12 units
  • 1,000 guests: 20-25 units

Adjust Your Count For These Factors

Event Duration:

  • Under 4 hours: Use base formula (1 per 50)
  • 4-8 hours: Add 20% more units
  • 8+ hours or all-day: Add 40% more units

Alcohol Service:

  • Beer/Wine served: Add 25% more units
  • Full bar: Add 30-40% more units
  • Open bar: Add 50% more units

Guest Demographics:

  • Majority male (60%+): Use base formula
  • Even split: Add 10% more units
  • Majority female (60%+): Add 20-25% more units

Wedding Porta Potty Calculator

Atlanta weddings typically need more units than standard events:

100-Guest Wedding (6 hours, alcohol)

  • Base need: 2 units (100 ÷ 50)
  • Add for duration: +20% = 2.4 units
  • Add for alcohol: +30% = 3.1 units
  • Final recommendation: 4 luxury units or 1 small restroom trailer

200-Guest Wedding (8 hours, open bar)

  • Base need: 4 units
  • Add for duration: +40% = 5.6 units
  • Add for alcohol: +50% = 8.4 units
  • Final recommendation: 1-2 luxury restroom trailers (serves 8-12 units worth)

Festival & Concert Guidelines

Large outdoor events in Atlanta Metro need more capacity:

  • Music Festival (all-day): 1 unit per 25 guests minimum
  • Food Festival: 1 unit per 35-40 guests (less alcohol, shorter stays)
  • Concert (3-5 hours): 1 unit per 40-50 guests
  • Marathon/5K: 1 unit per 75 participants (short duration)

What Happens If You Don't Have Enough?

Undersupplying porta potties leads to:

  • Long wait lines (15+ minutes)
  • Unsanitary conditions by end of event
  • Guest complaints and negative reviews
  • OSHA violations (construction sites - $7,000-$15,000 fines)
  • Health code violations (public events)

It's always better to have 1-2 extra units than to run short.
