How Much Does Porta Potty Rental Cost in Atlanta? 2026 Pricing Guide

Pricing Published 2026-03-08 • Updated March 2026

2026 Porta Potty Rental Costs in Atlanta

Quick Answer: Standard porta potty rental in Atlanta costs $159-$299 per month with weekly service included. Daily rentals run $75-$100, weekend rentals $150-$200. Luxury restroom trailers cost $1,200-$3,500 per weekend event.

Monthly Rental Pricing (Most Common)

Monthly rentals are the most economical option for construction sites and long-term events. Prices include weekly service visits in Metro Atlanta:

  • Standard Porta Potty: $159-$299/month - Basic unit, chemical flush, toilet paper holder
  • Deluxe Flushing Unit: $225-$350/month - Hand washing station built-in, better ventilation
  • ADA Compliant: $250-$400/month - Wheelchair accessible, larger interior, grab bars
  • Luxury Restroom Trailer: $2,500-$4,000/month - Multiple stalls, running water, climate control
  • Hand Washing Station (separate): $100-$175/month - Foot pump operated, soap dispenser

Short-Term Rental Rates

For events, parties, and temporary needs in Atlanta:

  • Daily Rate: $75-$100 for standard units
  • Weekly Rate: $100-$150 for standard units
  • Weekend Event: $150-$200 for standard, $1,200-$2,000 for luxury trailers
  • Multi-Day Event: Add $25-$50 per additional service visit

Additional Fees to Know

Atlanta porta potty companies typically charge these extra fees:

  • Delivery Fee: $25-$100 depending on distance from provider's location
  • Pickup Fee: Usually included in delivery, but confirm
  • Same-Day Delivery: $50-$150 rush fee (order before 11 AM)
  • Extra Service Visits: $50-$100 per visit beyond weekly schedule
  • Damage Fee: $100-$500 for vandalism or misuse
  • Cleaning/Sanitation Fee: $75-$150 for excessive mess

  • Marietta: $159-$299/month (competitive market, similar to Atlanta)

How to Get the Best Price

1. Book for Longer Terms - Monthly rentals are 40-50% cheaper per day than daily rentals

2. Call Multiple Companies - Prices vary $50-$100 between providers for identical units

3. Order in Advance - Same-day delivery adds $50-$150 to your cost

4. Bundle Services - Ordering multiple units often gets 10-15% discount

5. Ask About Specials - Many companies offer first-time customer discounts

What Affects Porta Potty Rental Cost?

Distance from Provider: Delivery fees increase with distance. Choose local companies when possible.

Rental Duration: Longer rentals = lower daily cost. A 6-month rental costs less per month than a 1-month rental.

Service Frequency: Weekly service is standard. Twice-weekly service adds $40-$75/month.

Unit Type: Standard units are cheapest. ADA units cost 20-30% more, luxury trailers cost 10-15x more.

Time of Year: Prices spike in summer (wedding season) and spring (construction season).

Hidden Costs to Watch For

Some Atlanta porta potty companies advertise low base rates but add fees later:

  • Environmental fees ($5-$15/month)
  • Fuel surcharges ($10-$25/delivery)
  • Weekend delivery fees ($25-$50 extra)
  • Permit fees (if required for public property)

Always ask for the "total out-the-door price" before booking.
