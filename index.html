Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before renting a porta potty in Metro Atlanta.

For construction sites, 2–3 business days is usually enough. For events — especially weddings, festivals, or anything requiring multiple units — book 2–4 weeks ahead. Several Atlanta companies offer same-day delivery for emergency situations, though availability varies by city and season.
The standard rule: 1 unit per 50 guests for a 4-hour event. For all-day events, plan 1 unit per 50 for every 4 hours. If alcohol is served, increase that by 25–40%. For construction sites, OSHA requires at minimum 1 unit per 10 workers on an 8-hour shift. Add at least 1 ADA-compliant unit if your event is open to the public.
Most standard rentals include delivery, setup, a weekly service/pump-out, and pickup. Hand sanitizer is usually included. Toilet paper is typically restocked during service visits. Some companies include a handwashing station at no extra charge — always ask upfront what's included to avoid surprises.
Yes — several companies in our directory offer same-day delivery across Metro Atlanta, including Patriot Portables, Porta Potty Rental Atlanta Direct, and select others. Call early in the day (before 10 AM) for best availability. Same-day delivery typically carries a small additional fee.
For placement on private property (your yard, a job site), generally no permit is required. For placement on a public sidewalk, street, or park — you'll likely need a city permit. In Atlanta and surrounding cities, your rental company can often advise you or help facilitate the permit process. Always check with your city or county first for events in public spaces.
Standard porta potties are self-contained, freestanding units with a chemical waste tank — practical and affordable for most uses. Restroom trailers are tow-behind units with flushing toilets, running water, climate control, and a finished interior closer to a real bathroom. Trailers are typically used for weddings, corporate events, and upscale gatherings where appearance matters. Trailers cost 3–10x more but make a significant difference in guest experience.
For construction sites: prioritize availability, service frequency, and price. For events: look for companies with luxury or deluxe options and a track record with events. For same-day needs: filter for companies with "Same-Day Delivery" badges. Always call at least 2 companies to compare quotes — prices can vary significantly, and many will match or beat a competitor's quote.
